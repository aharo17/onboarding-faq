Welcome Back Retirees

Access to Retiree Portal

Your log in username will now be your previously given username with the email domain added. For example, if you previously typed ‘jdoe’ into your username box, now your will type ‘jdoe@mesa-air.com’. You are now welcome to use either the Mesa Retiree Portal or Mesa Employee Intranet to access your Retiree Pass Bureau page.

Your log in password remains the same as your former password before this security update. There were no changes made to the password section. If you receive a screen referencing the reCAPTCHA, you are welcome to complete to verify that you are not a robot.

Microsoft Authenticator

One of the first steps of logging into your Mesa account is getting the Microsoft Authenticator Application installed on your device to access all of your necessary Microsoft Office Applications.

This is a Two-Factor Authentication method, similar to the text or email methods other companies use, it enhances security for your online account by providing one-time access codes. It is an essential tool to safeguard your digital identity and prevent unauthorized access. Watch our Training video to get started. Text and email are other options for Authentication.
